Achievements and Awards

Kevin Hart has achieved numerous awards and recognitions throughout his career. As of 2023, some of his notable awards and achievements include:
- Primetime Emmy Awards Nominee for Outstanding Actor in a Short Form Comedy or Drama Series for "Die Hart" in 2023.
- Grammy Awards: Nominee for Best Comedy Album for "Zero F***s Given" in 2022.
- Image Awards (NAACP):
- Nominee for Outstanding Character Voice-Over Performance (Motion Picture) for "DC League of Super-Pets" in 2023.
- Nominee for Outstanding Host in a Talk or News/Information (Series or Special) - Individual or Ensemble for "Hart to Heart" in 2023.
- Winner for Outstanding Actor in a Television Movie, Limited Series or Dramatic Special for "True Story" in 2022.
-Kids' Choice Awards, USA: Nominee for Favorite Voice from an Animated Movie (Male) for "DC League of Super-Pets" in 2023.
- MTV Movie + TV Awards: Winner of the Comedic Genius Award in 2015.
- People's Choice Awards, USA : Nominee for The Drama Movie of 2021. Additionally, Kevin Hart was named the recipient of the **Cannes Lions’ 2023 Entertainment Person of the Year award and will receive the Kennedy Center's Mark Twain Prize for lifetime achievement in American humor⁴.
For a comprehensive list of all his awards and nominations, you can visit his IMDb awards page.
